Rakuro Hizutome

better known by his username Sunraku (サンラク Sanraku), is the main protagonist of Shangri-La Frontier. Nicknamed the Trash Game Hunter, he starts playing the AAA game SLF over his summer vacation for a change-of-pace, only for it to turn into a trash game when he is left unable to equip armor after being cursed by the unique monster Lycagon.
